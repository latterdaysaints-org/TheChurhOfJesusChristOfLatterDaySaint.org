How It Works
Financial Support:   The Church recognizes that the financial burden from health challenges, losing a loved one, separation and some every day problems can be significant especially when you have to handle it alone, thus the church aims to ease that burden by offering financial assistance of up to $5,000.

Emotional / Spiritual Support:   The Church offers a range of Emotional and Spiritual Support services to help individuals who may be experiencing emotional challenges or struggling with their faith in the gospel of Jesus Christ. These services include individual counseling, therapy with licensed professional therapists, support groups, and guidance from LDS Church leaders.

One of the most significant benefits of these services is that they are available online, allowing individuals to receive support from the comfort of their own homes.
